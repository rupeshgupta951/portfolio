I'm a Frontend Developer based in Navi Mumbai with 3+ years of experience building production-ready web applications. My day-to-day revolves around Angular, TypeScript, and RxJS — but I think in terms of user flows, data states, and component contracts first.

I've shipped solutions across five different domains — Sales Dashboards, CRM, ERP, EdTech, and Event Booking — which has given me a strong instinct for building systems that non-technical stakeholders can actually use and engineers can actually maintain.

Beyond Angular, I work with React, Next.js for personal projects and SSR use cases, and actively use Cursor AI & GitHub Copilot to stay sharp without letting AI replace the thinking.

I care about clean architecture, responsive design, and code that future-you won't regret. Outside work, I'm always experimenting — whether it's a crypto dashboard, a CRUD app in React, or a calculator that actually works.

// how I work

What working with me looks like

01
I read requirements like a product person
Before writing a single line, I ask: who uses this, what do they need, and where could this fail? That thinking shows in the edge cases I catch before QA does.
02
Architecture before components
I design data flow, state boundaries, and service contracts upfront. Components are the output — not the starting point. This keeps codebases maintainable as they scale.
03
Delivery without dropping quality
Agile sprints, PR reviews, bug triage — I've worked in real teams under real deadlines. I know when to ship and when to refactor, and I communicate the tradeoffs clearly.
